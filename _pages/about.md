I'm currently a **Computer Science** DPhil student at the **University of Oxford**, supervised by [Professor Ronald Clark](https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/people/ronald.clark/). I'm fortunate to be funded by the [Portuguese Foundation for Science and Technology](https://www.fct.pt/en/).

I'm doing research in **Computer Vision**, specifically in **3D Vision** trying to provide image generative models with the capacity to allow users to control the properties of images. I've have also worked on evaluating and mitigating societal bias in vision-language models.

I did my masters and undergrad at Instituto Superior Técnico.
