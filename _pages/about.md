I'm currently a first-year DPhil student in **Computer Science** at the **University of Oxford**, supervised by [Professor Ronald Clark](https://www.cs.ox.ac.uk/people/ronald.clark/). I'm fortunate to be funded by the [Portuguese Foundation for Science and Technology](https://www.fct.pt/en/).

I'm doing research in **Computer Vision**, specifically in **3D Vision** trying to provide image generative models with the capacity to allow users to control the properties of images. I've also worked on evaluating and mitigating societal bias in vision-language models.

I did my masters and undergrad at Instituto Superior Técnico.

- **May 2023**: We just published [GenSynth](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.15407), a balanced dataset built with synthetic contrast sets to understand and mitigate bias in vision-language models.
